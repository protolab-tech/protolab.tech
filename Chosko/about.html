Our Story

Born from the streets, CHOSKO represents the fusion of skateboarding culture and contemporary fashion. We believe in creating clothing that not only looks good but can handle the demands of skateboarding.

Our mission is simple: to make you shine - Siamo nati per brillare. Each piece in our collection is crafted with attention to detail and designed to make you stand out, whether you're hitting the park or cruising through the city.
